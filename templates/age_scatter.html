Age vs. Survival Scatter Plot

This scatter plot visualizes the relationship between Age and survival.

What Does This Graph Show?

The scatter plot represents individual passengers, where color distinguishes survivors (1) from non-survivors (0).
Due to **fewer passengers in the 50+ age group**, survival rates in that range appear to decrease.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers aged **below 30** had a **higher survival rate**.
  • Survival rates were **lowest between ages 30-50**.
  • Although survival appears to drop for **50+ passengers**, this is likely influenced by their **smaller sample size**.
