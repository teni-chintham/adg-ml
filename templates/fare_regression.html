Fare vs. Survival Regression Plot

This regression plot helps analyze the relationship between ticket price (Fare) and survival probability.

What Does This Graph Show?

The regression line predicts survival likelihood based on fare price.
The trend line suggests how increasing fare affects survival probability.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers with higher fares generally had a higher survival probability.
  • The logistic regression curve models survival as a probability.
  • Lower fares are associated with a lower chance of survival.
