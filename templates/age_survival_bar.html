Age vs. Survival Rate

This visualization explores how Age influenced survival rates on the Titanic.

What Does This Bar Plot Show?

The bar chart represents the average survival rate across different age groups.
A U-shaped trend is observed, meaning survival rates varied significantly with age.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers under 30 had a relatively higher survival rate.
  • The survival rate decreased for those aged 30 to 50.
  • Passengers over 50 had slightly better survival chances compared to those in the 30-50 range.
  • The U-shaped pattern suggests that younger individuals and elderly passengers had better survival prospects than middle-aged adults.
