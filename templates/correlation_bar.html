Survivor Correlation

This bar chart visualizes how different features correlate with survival on the Titanic. Its a more descriptive viewing of heatmap for target variable

What is this Chart Showing?

The Survivor Correlation Bar Chart shows how each variable in the dataset relates to the likelihood of survival.
Bars extending further indicate stronger positive or negative correlations.

Key Takeaways:

Looking at the correlation values, we can determine that the factors with the highest impact on survival include:

  • Sex (strongest correlation with survival)
  • Pclass (social class played a significant role)
  • Fare (fare made an impact on higher survival chances)
