Pclass vs. Survival

This bar plot examines the relationship between Passenger Class (Pclass) and Survival Rate on the Titanic.

What Does This Bar Plot Show?

The graph represents the average survival rate for different Pclass categories.
A lower Pclass number (higher class) is associated with a higher probability of survival.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers in Pclass 1 had the highest survival rate, while those in Pclass 3 had the lowest.
  • There is an almost linear trend where survival decreases as Pclass increases.
  • This reinforces the idea that wealthier passengers in higher classes had better access to lifeboats and safety measures.
