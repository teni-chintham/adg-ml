Fare vs. Survival Scatter Plot

This scatter plot visualizes how ticket price (Fare) relates to survival.

What Does This Graph Show?

The scatter plot represents individual passengers.
The color distinguishes between those who survived (1) and those who did not (0).

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers with higher fares generally had a greater survival rate.
  • Lower fare passengers were less likely to survive, reinforcing the link between fare and class.
  • Since survival is binary (0 or 1), the density of points is key for regression analysis.
  • There is an outlier above $500 that is too isolated to significantly impact the overall trend.
