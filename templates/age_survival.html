Age vs. Survival Rate

This visualization explores how Age influenced survival rates on the Titanic.

What Does This Graph Show?

The bar chart represents the survival rate at different age ranges.
The red trendline highlights the overall survival trend across ages.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers under 30 had a relatively higher survival rate.
  • Survival rates dropped for those aged 30 to 50, indicating a lower chance of survival.
  • Passengers over 50 had a slight rebound in survival rates.
  • This creates a U-shaped trend, where younger and older passengers had better survival chances than middle-aged adults.
