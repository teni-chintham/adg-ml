Sex vs Survival

This bar plot examines the relationship between Sex and Survival Rate on the Titanic.

What Does This Bar Plot Show?

The graph represents the average survival rate for different Sex amounts.
Higher Sex values often indicate a higher probability of survival.

Conclusions from this Analysis:

By analyzing the Sex column, we can conclude:

  • Passengers who paid higher Sex had a better survival rate.
  • Lower Sex values correlate with a lower survival rate.
  • This suggests that passengers in higher classes had better chances of survival.
