Sex vs. Survival Scatter Plot

This scatter plot visualizes how ticket price (sex) relates to survival.

What Does This Graph Show?

The scatter plot represents individual passengers.
The color distinguishes between those who survived (1) and those who did not (0).

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers with higher Sex had a higher survival rate.
  • Lower Sex passengers were less likely to survive.
  • The separation between points highlights the correlation between class and survival.
