Pclass vs. Survival Rate

This visualization explores the relationship between Passenger Class (Pclass) and survival rate on the Titanic.

What Does This Graph Show?

The bar chart displays the survival rate for each passenger class (Pclass).
The red trendline highlights the overall pattern in survival rates across different classes.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers in Pclass 1 had the highest survival rate, while those in Pclass 3 had the lowest.
  • The trendline confirms a strong negative correlation between Pclass number and survival probability.
  • Lower-class passengers faced significantly lower survival rates, likely due to limited access to lifeboats and safety measures.
