Fare vs. Survival Rate

This visualization helps us understand the relationship between the ticket price (Fare) and survival rate.

What Does This Graph Show?

The bar chart represents the survival rate at different fare prices.
The red trendline shows an overall trend in the survival rate based on fare values.

Key Takeaways:

  • Passengers who paid higher fares had a higher survival rate.
  • The trendline suggests a positive correlation between fare and survival probability.
  • Lower fare passengers had a significantly lower survival rate.
  • Ignore the outlier its too far away from the main data to have any significance
