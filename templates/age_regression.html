Age vs. Survival Regression Plot

This regression plot examines the relationship between Age and survival probability.

What Does This Graph Show?

The regression line estimates the probability of survival based on Age.
A **U-shaped trend** emerges, but survival probability appears to **decline for passengers over 50**, possibly due to their **lower representation** in the dataset.

Key Takeaways:

  • Children and younger passengers had a **higher** survival probability.
  • Passengers aged **30-50** had the **lowest** survival rates.
  • Survival probability **seems to decrease for 50+ passengers**, but this is likely due to their **lower numbers in the dataset**.
